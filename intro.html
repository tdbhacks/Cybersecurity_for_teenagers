Introduction

As an Internet user, you face several threats every time you receive an email, open a website, or even click a link. While the Internet is a very powerful resource, you need to realize that it comes with some risks too. For instance, take a look at the widget on the right to see how easy it is to track down a website user.

This website is designed to serve as an introduction to Cybersecurity, so that you can freely use and explore the world wide web without risking to compromise your safety.

The guide is divided into three fondumental sections (passwords, scams, and fake news) that will help you protect yourself against hackers and enjoy all the possibilities offered by the Internet.
